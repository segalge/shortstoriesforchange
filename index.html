Erikson's Stages of Development

"We have found Erik Erikson’s schema of development across the life cycle to provide useful definitions of positive and negative change at each of his 'eight stages of man.' ... [detailed descriptions of each stage] ... Of course, the writers of such stories may not be thinking in any such terms. Their gift to us has been to infuse their characters and situations with such intensity and clarity that, if we let them—that is, if we take them seriously—they become part of our lives and remain with us, sometimes even more clearly than the people we know. They manage to take life itself and present it not only with color and drama but also with structure, so that it may seem more real than our actual daily experiences."
